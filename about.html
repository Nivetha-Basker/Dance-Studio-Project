A Little Bit About Dance Studio!

Hi there! Welcome to our Dance Studio .


At Dance Studio!, you can opt to learn different styles of yoga and dance through regular weekday classes and specialised weekend workshops conducted by experts in each field. Hatha, Ashtanga and the Mysore-style are some of the yoga practices on offer at TAS. Dance enthusiasts can choose from multiple styles including hip-hop, house, Bollywood, street jazz and heels dance. In addition to workouts, DanceGo! offers courses and classes in specialised fields such as meditation and healing, medical yoga, and pre-natal and post- natal yoga. Dance Studio! is a pet-friendly studio with something to offer to every type of fitness-minded person and pooch!


Since 2002, we've dedicated ourselves to helping dance studios grow their businesses through professionally designed websites. Ours is a team of motivated and experienced individuals with solid backgrounds in dance and the teaching of dance. We speak your language, and we're here to help!


As for me, I'm a former dancer/dance teacher/choreographer turned website designer & marketing consultant. I too have a dance related business and have experienced the highs, lows, frustrations and excitements of growing it gracefully.
